TYPES OF DRUNK DRIVER ACCIDENTS

No two drunk driver accidents are exactly alike, but qualified and experienced drunk driving accident lawyer Steven C. Lee can handle all types of these cases.

DRUNK DRIVER CAR COLLISIONS

By far the most common type of drunk driver accidents, collisions between drunk drivers and other passenger vehicles happen often in Austin. Even if you think your injuries are minor, be sure to seek medical attention and call a qualified drunk driving accident lawyer ASAP.

DRUNK DRIVERS & PEDESTRIANS

Especially in downtown Austin, pedestrians are at risk of being injured by drunk drivers. The results are often serious, if not fatal. Texas courts take these types of drunk driver accidents extremely seriously. If you've been injured, Steven C. Lee & Associates can help.

DRUNK DRIVERS & BICYCLISTS

It's no secret that Austinites love riding bikes. Bicycle accidents involving cars are bad enough, but when drunk drivers are involved, 10mph collisions can easily turn into accidents at 30 or 40mph. Always wear a helmet, and immediately seek legal representation if you're a victim.

HIT AND RUN ACCIDENTS

Drunk drivers are notorious for making poor decisions. Among the most heinous of those decisions is fleeing from the scene of a drunk driving accident. If this has happened to you or a loved one, you should hire a qualified drunk driving accident lawyer to help track down those responsible and bring them to justice.
